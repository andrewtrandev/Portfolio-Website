Doubts & Insecurities

29/3/2020

2:36am - Dump everything out of my brain, anxiety about not being good enough in this course. That I'm not smart, that I'm not working hard enough, that there are so many amazing smart people that are just better than me, and that makes me question my own worth and ability. But this is an unbalanced view right? There's always going to be someone better than you, can I be happy with who I am today versus who I was yesterday?, if I'm studying and improving even by 1% isn't that enough? Do I need self-flagellation to push me or does that hinder me, make me more fearful, more withdrawn, less the good side of me and more the bad parts. But maybe even this is an unbalanced view, the human is both the good and bad mixed together, these bad parts have an evolutionary basis and there's a way I can use them to my advantage.
