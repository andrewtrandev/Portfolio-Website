About me

While working as a data processor, I made a bunch of simple scripts to help me at my job. I realised after using them that they were awesome and saved me heaps of time, I was comfortable at my job but neither challenged or thrilled about it. This got me questioning "What am I even doing with my life?"

Intrigued about doing something different with my life, I thought for awhile about all the different things I could do. I had varied ideas including becoming a personal trainer, physiotherapist and nurse. After thinking about my scripts I thought I would have a look into coding, after doing some resarch, I found Coder Academy who were promoting a 6-month intensive bootcamp. Thrilled by the idea of a super tough course and a new challenge, I enrolled.

This bootcamp has definitely been one of the toughest things I have ever done, but that question of "What am I even doing with my life?" has faded into the background and I wake up now, surprisingly, thrilled to boot up my pc and start coding.
